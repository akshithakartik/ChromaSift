Title: International union of pure and applied chemistry

Abstract: The role of the International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry is described, including detail on the central role that it plays in establishing and maintaining international consensus and consistency in chemical nomenclature, terminology and units. It sets standards on standardized methodology and evaluation of data, and publishes tables of critically evaluated data. Expansion of these activities into the digital domain is a key current activity. The input to toxicology through Division VII, Chemistry and Human Health, is described in some detail.
